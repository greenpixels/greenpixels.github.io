About me

Hey there! My name is Sven and I am a game developer from Germany. I've been developing games since 2016. I grew up playing games on Gameboy Advance, Nintendo 64 and Playstation 2. Some of my favorite games from then were The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, Dragon Quest Monsters, Pokemon Emerald Edition and Digimon World.

The first game projects I created were following YouTube-Tutorials using JavaFX, which was also my first dive into programming altogether. That was when I ultimately decided that programming is the career path I wanted to take. In 2018 I applied for an apprenticeship in software-development and have been working full-time in that field ever since.

Despite working full-time, my love for game development has not wavered even a little bit. I've been creating game projects on and off in my free time, tested out several game engines, libaries and programming languages and used the knowledge from my day-job as a strong foundation to strengthen my game-dev journey. Also, as a kid one of my favorite hobbies was drawing - with game development I was able to channel that creative hobby by creating pixel art for most my projects. You can see some of my pixel art work down below.

In 2023 I released my first (free) Steam game - Dungeon Mage. Dungeon Mage was a great first step into turning my hobby into something more serious. I started creating a Discord, keeping in touch with the community and created more game projects over the next years.

The most recent project I am working on is Gamblers Table where I am the programmer and also contribute partially as an artist - together with Bossforge as the main artist.
